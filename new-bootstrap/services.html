Classic Beds: These beds are a great starting point for someone new to tanning, or an excellent way to maintain an established tan. Our beds use top-quality bulbs to ensure even exposure and to maximize results.

VHR Beds & Booths: Our Very High Output with Reflector (VHR) lamps give you a tanning experience similar to the Mediterranean sun during mid day. These booths often give the user a better tan because it is easier to move while tanning. The VHO-R lamps, which have a more effective reflector built inside the lamp itself, forces all the light to be focused out the front of the lamp, reducing lost UV from phase cancellation.

Additionally, there are no pressure points when tanning inside a booth. A person using a tanning bed is supported by the acrylic, and in these areas the blood flow is reduced. Melanin production is somewhat reduced in these areas leading to a tan that is not completely even. For most individuals, this isn't very obvious but certain individuals will experience circular areas with slight but noticeably less tan in those pressure areas.

VHR w/ Facials: Our Very High Output with Reflector (VHR) lamps give you a tanning experience similar to the Mediterranean sun during mid day. In addition to these highly-efficient tanning sessions, we also offer facials to improve your tan from the neck up and ensure healthy-looking skin.

Red Light Therapy: Photo Rejuvenation works at the cellular level. Each cell contains a number of power plants, called mitochondria. The function of these power plants is to produce ATP (adenosine triphosphate), the form of energy that can be used by the cell to function properly. The Red and Infrared spectrum of the electromagnetic radiation are used for Photo Rejuvenation because they are well absorbed by the collector surfaces of the mitochondria as an additional source of energy. The cellular power plants can thus produce more ATP. ATP is the cellular energy or fuel the skin cells strive after and need. A sufficiently high supply of cellular energy enables our skin cells to work under optimum conditions and is the essential prerequisite to ensure a successful self-healing process.
